Intern Sip Products

Global Discord Community

  • 1. Different channels for different industries (i.e. tech, health, entertainment
  • 2. Users select interests or all options ("all" for those who don't know what career they want), to get access and personalized support for it in the respective channels
  • 3. Receive access to mentors from Marvel, Microsoft, local hospitals, and more!

Mentor Sip

  • 1. Interns Sip hosts projects for all industries and any students can apply to gain experience on their resume. For this, Intern Sip is a MENTOR and guides/supports the projects.
  • 2. We have dedicated projects and roles for students including those with little/no experience and people with accessibility needs.
  • 3. **Upcoming Projects**
    • 1. Animated short film -> students can come together to direct and create a film
    • 2. Tech for goood -> developers, UX, business, PM, come together to create a product
    • 3. Designhathon -> launch a 24-48 hour design competition

Partner Sip

  • 1. While Intern Sip hosts projects, if students are creating an event/project and want our support and guidance, they can apply to be a partner for our resources.
  • 2. This includes funding, access to our mentors/contacts, sponsorship guidance, we host a workshop at their event, etc.
